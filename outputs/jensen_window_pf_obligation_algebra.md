# Jensen-Window PF Obligation Algebra

Date: 2026-07-05

Status: exact algebra and countermodel gate. This is not a proof of
Jensen-window PF-infinity, Laguerre-Polya membership, RH, or `Lambda <= 0`;
it records low-degree obligations and proof-safety failures for the
Jensen-window route.

## Purpose

The Jensen-window PF target asks that, for every degree `d` and shift `n`, the
finite sequence:

```text
B^{d,n,0}_j = binom(d,j) A_{n+j}(0)
```

be PF-infinity. Equivalently, the infinite banded Toeplitz matrix generated by
that finite sequence must be totally nonnegative.

This gate records the first exact low-degree obligations. It separates:

```text
degree-2 contact with signed-Hankel
degree-3 and degree-4 Jensen-window Toeplitz obligations
finite low-order checks that still do not imply PF-infinity
```

## Degree 2 Exact Contact

For the degree-2 window:

```text
B = (A_n, 2 A_{n+1}, A_{n+2})
```

the Jensen discriminant is:

```text
4(A_{n+1}^2 - A_n A_{n+2}).
```

The signed-Hankel relation is:

```text
A_{n+1}^2 - A_n A_{n+2}
= -det [[A_n, A_{n+1}],
        [A_{n+1}, A_{n+2}]].
```

So degree 2 is the exact contact point: the `m = 1` signed-Hankel condition is
precisely the degree-2 Jensen-window PF threshold.

In Toeplitz language, the contiguous tridiagonal minors obey:

```text
D_m = 2 A_{n+1} D_{m-1} - A_n A_{n+2} D_{m-2}
D_0 = 1
D_1 = 2 A_{n+1}
```

The all-`m` nonnegativity threshold is again:

```text
A_{n+1}^2 - A_n A_{n+2} >= 0.
```

This is why a single small Toeplitz minor is not the right endpoint even in
degree 2; the PF target is an all-minor condition for the banded Toeplitz
matrix.

## Degree 3 New Obligations

For:

```text
B = (A_n, 3 A_{n+1}, 3 A_{n+2}, A_{n+3})
```

selected low-order Toeplitz minors include:

```text
-3(A_n A_{n+2} - 3 A_{n+1}^2)
-A_n A_{n+3} + 9 A_{n+1} A_{n+2}
-3(A_{n+1} A_{n+3} - 3 A_{n+2}^2)
```

and the contiguous `3 x 3` minors:

```text
A_n^2 A_{n+3} - 18 A_n A_{n+1} A_{n+2} + 27 A_{n+1}^3
A_n A_{n+3}^2 - 18 A_{n+1} A_{n+2} A_{n+3} + 27 A_{n+2}^3
```

The cubic Jensen discriminant is:

```text
-27(A_n^2 A_{n+3}^2
    - 6 A_n A_{n+1} A_{n+2} A_{n+3}
    + 4 A_n A_{n+2}^3
    + 4 A_{n+1}^3 A_{n+3}
    - 3 A_{n+1}^2 A_{n+2}^2).
```

This is not the same as a signed reshaped-Hankel determinant. A bridge theorem
has to explain why the actual Xi/Phi coefficient structure supplies this
Jensen-window PF condition.

## Degree 4 New Obligations

For:

```text
B = (A_n, 4 A_{n+1}, 6 A_{n+2}, 4 A_{n+3}, A_{n+4})
```

selected `2 x 2` Toeplitz minors include:

```text
-2(3 A_n A_{n+2} - 8 A_{n+1}^2)
-4(A_n A_{n+3} - 6 A_{n+1} A_{n+2})
-A_n A_{n+4} + 16 A_{n+1} A_{n+3}
-4(4 A_{n+1} A_{n+3} - 9 A_{n+2}^2)
-4(A_{n+1} A_{n+4} - 6 A_{n+2} A_{n+3})
-2(3 A_{n+2} A_{n+4} - 8 A_{n+3}^2)
```

selected contiguous `3 x 3` Toeplitz minors include:

```text
4(A_n^2 A_{n+3} - 12 A_n A_{n+1} A_{n+2} + 16 A_{n+1}^3)

-2(3 A_n A_{n+2} A_{n+4}
   - 8 A_n A_{n+3}^2
   - 8 A_{n+1}^2 A_{n+4}
   + 96 A_{n+1} A_{n+2} A_{n+3}
   - 108 A_{n+2}^3)

4(A_{n+1} A_{n+4}^2 - 12 A_{n+2} A_{n+3} A_{n+4}
  + 16 A_{n+3}^3)
```

These are Jensen-window PF obligations. They are adjacent to signed-Hankel
evidence, but they are not identical to the reshaped-Hankel minors currently
certified.
